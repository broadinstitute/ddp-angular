About the Study

Our goal is to watch the infection rate of COVID-19 in Massachusetts over time. Some communities have higher infection rates than others. We want to understand this and provide linkages to care for those living in these high-risk communities.

Questions we are trying to answer:

  • •How common it is to have COVID-19 without symptoms?
  • •Do people become immune to COVID-19 after they have it?
  • •How can researchers test many people while they are at home?
