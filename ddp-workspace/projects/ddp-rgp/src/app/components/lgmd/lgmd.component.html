How You Can Participate

Tell us your story

Click apply to tell us about the history of LGMD in your family. The information will be reviewed by our study team, and eligible families will be invited to participate. We will contact you to arrange a detailed discussion about the study and send you a consent form.

Submit a sample

Once enrolled, we’ll mail a pre-paid sample collection kit directly to you and your family. We’ll also ask you more about medical history and ask for copies of medical records, including genetic test results.

Discover

Through DNA sequencing, we may discover the genetic cause of the LGMD in your family and will work with you and your doctors to return important results to you. We will also share de-identified data broadly with the scientific community to empower discoveries in rare and undiagnosed disease, including LGMD.
