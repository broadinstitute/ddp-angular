Examples of primary brain cancers: glioblastoma, astrocytoma, oligodendroglioma, and medulloblastoma.

What’s this project about?

The Brain Cancer Project is part of Count Me In, a nonprofit organization that’s pursuing a new approach to cancer research - bringing patients and researchers together as partners to accelerate discoveries.

Patients with brain cancer are empowered to contribute to breakthroughs and increase the pace of biomedical research, no matter where they live. By discovering the genes and the variants that drive this disease and sharing this data, we hope insights can be gained to develop more effective therapies.

To learn more about Count Me In, our mission, and other projects and published research, visit our site.
